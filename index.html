What is this?

Pokémon Team Builder is an online tool to help you plan and build a strong and well-balanced Pokémon team, for both in-game and competitive use. It has smart filtering options, is capable of adding attacks to Pokémon, and has useful features such as team sharing, team importing, and links to Pokédex entries on Serebii. The Type Charts show you how your team is balanced and if it is well covered, both offensively and defensively. The Pokémon and movesets are updated for the 8th generation, so it is compatible with the new Sword & Shield games and with the Pokémon Bank update.
